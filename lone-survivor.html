About Lone Survivor

A First Person Zombie Apocalypse Shooter

Being over ambitious, scientists were conducting a secret and very risky experiment, whose result were catastrophic. The experiment infected the humans and turned them into zombies. The infection spread rapidly. Military personnel were mobilised to get a hold of the situation in the cities but they got over run very quickly. That's where the player find itself in the game.

The game is developed using Unreal Engine 4 and C++. This game features a soldier (player) trying to survive and escape the zombie infested city. The assets used for the game were obtained from Unreal Marketplace and other free game resources websites.


Some Gameplay Elements

  • Player's walk speed decreasing with decreasing health
  • Player cannot move while shooting and vice versa
  • Zombie Headshot Kills
  • Rewards for killing the zombies
  • Difficulty System - almost every thing changes with the Difficulty. The zombie hitpoints, rewards for killing zombies, number of headshots it takes to kill the zombie.
  • Difficulty decreases if the player is having a hard time
